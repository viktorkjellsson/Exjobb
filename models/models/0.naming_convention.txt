How the models are named: 

lstm_model_name_inputdim_hiddendim_hiddenlayers_learningrate_dropout

e.g. lstm_model_loopB_20_128_2_10_0.001_0.4
	Name: lstm_model_loopB
	Input dimension: 20 channels
	Hidden dimension: 128
	Hidden layers: 2 layers
	Learning rate: 0.001
	Dropout: 0.4

The rest of the model parameters as well as training performance can be found in the log.json files.
